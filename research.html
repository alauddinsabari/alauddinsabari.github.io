Research

My research sits at the intersection of applied statistics, machine learning, and software/security analysis. I aim to build AI systems that are reliable, interpretable, and deployable.

Current Focus

  • Android security & privacy: large-scale analysis of app permissions and behavior signals.
  • LLM-centered workflows: using LLMs for labeling, summarization, and evaluation of security-relevant artifacts.
  • Interpretable modeling: SHAP / feature attribution and uncertainty-aware decision support.
  • Applied pipelines: data collection, preprocessing, model training, evaluation, and reporting.
