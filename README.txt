How to use:
1) Unzip the folder.
2) Open index.html in your browser (double-click).
3) To host:
   - GitHub Pages: upload the folder contents to a repo and enable Pages.
   - Netlify/Vercel: drag-and-drop the folder or connect your repo.

Update content:
- Edit the HTML pages directly (index.html, research.html, publications.html, projects.html, talk.html, cv.html).
- Replace the CV at: assets/Alauddin_Sabari_CV.pdf (keep the filename).
- Add your video:
  - talk.html: replace VIDEO_ID in the YouTube iframe,
    OR add assets/media/presentation.mp4 and use the HTML5 video option.


New in v2:
- Add your photo:
  1) Put a square photo at assets/img/profile.jpg (recommended 600x600).
  2) In index.html, change the img src from assets/img/profile_placeholder.svg to assets/img/profile.jpg

- Light/Dark theme toggle:
  Click the "Light/Dark" button in the navbar. It remembers your choice in the browser.

- Landing page video:
  index.html has a YouTube embed. Replace VIDEO_ID with your video ID (or swap to a local mp4 if you prefer).
